Batman's Technology

Batman uses high-tech gadgets, the iconic Batmobile, and his armored suit to outsmart his enemies. His gear is built at Wayne Enterprises and often upgraded after each battle.

  • Batmobile: A sleek, tank-like vehicle with weapons, boosters, and armor plating.
  • Utility Belt: Equipped with batarangs, smoke bombs, and hacking tools.
  • Grapple Gun: Allows Batman to scale buildings quickly.
  • Detective Mode: Advanced scanning system built into his cowl.
  • EMP Gun: Temporarily disables enemy electronics.

Uniting the Superheroes

After sensing a growing threat from beyond Earth, Batman took the lead to form a team of powerful allies. He reached out to heroes across the world to defend humanity.

  • Aquaman: Ruler of Atlantis and master of oceanic forces.
